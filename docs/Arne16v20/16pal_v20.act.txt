16pal_v20.act - Photoshop can load this file, but it's just 772 bytes of raw data. According to myself at the point of writing this, the format is 256 colors * 3 R,G,B bytes = 768 + three bytes ending the file, 16, 255, 255, where 16 is the amount of colors actually used (the rest are zeroed).
